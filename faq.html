Frequently Asked Questions

What is League of Legends Boosting?

League of Legends boosting is a service that helps players increase their in-game rank or ELO through the assistance of a professional booster. Whether you want to reach a higher rank or get better performance in ranked games, boosting can help you achieve your goals.

How does the boosting process work?

After you place an order, our professional booster will work on your account to help you reach your desired rank. You can track the progress through your dashboard and even chat with your booster for updates.

Is my account safe during the boost?

Yes, we take your account security very seriously. All of our boosters follow strict security measures to ensure your account remains safe and your privacy is respected.

How long does it take to complete a boost?

The time it takes depends on your current rank and the desired rank. Typically, a boost can take anywhere from a few hours to several days. Once you place your order, you'll receive an estimated completion time.

Can I play on my account during the boost?

We recommend not playing on your account during the boost to avoid any interruptions or potential delays. However, if you need to play, please let your booster know in advance.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a refund if the boost is not completed within the agreed timeframe. Please refer to our refund policy for full details.
